Arts


An art is a diverse range of one activities. It is a human expression influenced by the culture and driven by the human impulses. It can be described as visual arts, literature arts, performing arts, decorative art and tangible arts.



Courses You Can Do After 12th Arts:-


BMS- Bachelor of Management Science click me

BMS full form Bachelor of Management Studies is a 3 year undergraduate course.The course is designed to study analytical aspects of Business Management and get in-depth knowledge in several managerial or business-related subjects such as human resource management, economics, marketing, business analytics etc.


BBA-Bachelor of Business Administration click me

BBA Course is a three year undergraduate business management course that imparts managerial and entrepreneurial skills to students through BBA Subjects. BBA courses are available in various specializations such as Finance, Marketing and HR Management. Bachelor of Business Administration is availble to students from all streams like Arts, Science and Commerce.

BFA- Bachelor of Fine Arts click me

BFA or Bachelor of Fine Arts is an undergraduate degree course which deals with the study of Visual or Performing Arts. The course is sometimes also referred to as Bachelor of Visual Arts (BVA) in which the study of Visual Arts includes subjects such as painting, sculpture, photography, literature, animation, etc

BEM- Bachelor of Event Management Integrated Law Course- BA + LL.B click me

Bachelor of Event Management is the process of planning of project management to design and establishment of small and large-scale personal or organizational activities such as concerts, conventions, ceremonies, weddings, etc. While planning/ organising an event, it is important to determine the targeted audience, establish the theme according to the event, organize the arrangements and give invites to the event to be scheduled. Subsequently, at the master's level, applicants can pursue elaborate programs in the field of event management specialization.

BJMC- Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication click me

Bachelor of Journalism & Mass Communication (BJMC) is an undergraduate program that provides aspirants with knowledge about various journalistic skills in mass media such as newspapers, television, radio, internet etc.
